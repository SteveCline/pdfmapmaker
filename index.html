About PDF Map Maker

Print Ain't Dead Yet!

Let's face it, we still need paper and PDF maps. Meet the web's best PDF Map Maker. Pick a layout, pick a basemap, add your own geoJSON data, style it, add a title and download a PDF map to print. Need to create some data to use on your map? Use my free editor vector.rocks to create and download your own geo-data.

PDF Map Maker is a project created and maintained by Stephen Cline. Hit me up on Twitter to share the cool maps you make!
